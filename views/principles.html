What would happen

If you grew an organisation from the ground up, based firmly on agile values and principles?

Human

We focus on meeting people's needs. By subordinating the work system to the people we have a far more healthy and high-functioning system than if we were to treat people as parts of a machine.

Emergent

We move forward through short experiments with courageous feedback. Ideas grow, becoming things that adapt and sometimes that die. We become stronger, evolving and adapting, through the process.

Independent

We are not a partner to any other service provider, we never resell 3rd party licenses, we don't aim to get bums on seats. We are driven by providing the best possible advice and guidance for each context.

Ecosystem

We harness network effects - by actively growing ourselves and the networks of people around us we raise ourselves up, uplifting our whole community, and ultimately our whole country, continent and world.
